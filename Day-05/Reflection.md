# Reflection

## 1. What makes a good prompt?

A good prompt is clear, specific, and provides enough context. The more detailed the prompt, the better the AI can understand the request and generate useful responses.

---

## 2. What mistakes lead to poor AI responses?

Poor AI responses usually happen when prompts are vague, incomplete, or lack sufficient details. Ambiguous questions can lead to inaccurate or irrelevant answers.

---

## 3. Which prompting technique gave the best results?

Role-Based Prompting and Step-by-Step Prompting gave the best results. They produced more detailed, organized, and accurate explanations compared to basic prompts.

---

## 4. How can AI help developers become more productive?

AI can help developers by explaining concepts, generating code, debugging programs, creating documentation, suggesting project ideas, and automating repetitive tasks. This saves time and improves productivity.

---

## 5. What limitations did you notice in AI responses?

AI can sometimes provide incorrect information, misunderstand the prompt, or generate incomplete answers. It may also lack real-world context and therefore requires human verification.

---

## Conclusion

Through this activity, I learned that the quality of AI responses depends heavily on the quality of prompts. Well-structured prompts produce more accurate and useful results, making AI a valuable tool for learning and development.
